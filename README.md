Author: Aleksei Sadovnikov  
Location: Kingston, Jamaica  

## Overview

This repository contains research materials for the **Information-Dynamic Theory (IDT)** programme.

The theory studies dynamical systems evolving on a Fisher-information manifold under natural gradient flow:

dω/dτ = −G⁻¹ ∇Φ

Key research directions:

- Structural inertia
- Fisher-Rao information geometry
- Natural-gradient dynamics
- Dimensional stability of bound regimes
- G-active relaxation dynamics
- Emergent informational time

## Main Papers

Included research papers:

- Structural Inertia and Relaxation Geometry
- G-Active Structural Inertia in Neural Networks
- Dimensional Structural Inertia Law
- IDT-D3 Dimensional Selection
